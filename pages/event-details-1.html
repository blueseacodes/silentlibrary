Children's Story Time

Join us for a magical hour of storytelling, where imaginations soar and young minds discover the joy of reading!

Date: 15 July 2025

Time: 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Location: Children's Corner, Silent Library

Target Age: 3 - 7 years old

Host: Ms. Lily Chen (Children's Librarian)

Activities: Story reading, sing-alongs, and a small craft activity.

Our story time sessions are designed to be interactive and engaging, fostering a love for books and reading from a young age. Each session features a selection of captivating stories, followed by fun songs and a simple craft that ties into the day's theme. It's a perfect opportunity for children to socialize and develop early literacy skills.

  • Engaging stories read aloud by our friendly librarians
  • Interactive songs and rhymes
  • Simple, fun craft activity related to the stories
  • A welcoming environment for young readers
