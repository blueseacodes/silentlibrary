Annual Used Book Fair

Our highly anticipated Annual Used Book Fair is back! Discover hidden gems, classic reads, and popular fiction at unbeatable prices.

Dates: 20 August - 22 August 2025

Time: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM Daily

Location: Silent Library Auditorium (Ground Floor)
