Local Author Meet & Greet

An exclusive opportunity to meet acclaimed author Celeste Ng! She will discuss her novel "Little Fires Everywhere", share insights into her writing process, and answer questions from the audience.

Date: 22 July 2025

Time: 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM

Location: Main Hall, Silent Library

Featured Author: Celeste Ng

Book: "Little Fires Everywhere"

  • Hear directly from the author about her writing process
  • Participate in a Q&A session

  • Network with other book lovers
