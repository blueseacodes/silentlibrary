The Silent Forest

Author: Alex Green

Genre: Mystery, Thriller

Published: March 12, 2022

ISBN: 978-0-123456-78-9

In the heart of an ancient, sprawling forest, a dark secret lies buried. When a young detective is called to investigate a series of inexplicable disappearances, they uncover a chilling truth that has been whispered through generations. "The Silent Forest" is a gripping thriller that will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very last page.
