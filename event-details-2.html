Introduction to Digital Art

Discover the exciting world of digital art! This workshop is perfect for beginners looking to explore creative tools and techniques on a digital canvas.

Date: 19 July 2025

Time: 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Location: Library Media Lab (Level 2)

Instructor: Ms. Emily Wong

Materials: Laptops and drawing tablets will be provided.

Prerequisites: None, suitable for all skill levels.

During this hands-on session, you will learn about:

  • Basic digital drawing tools and software interfaces (e.g., Procreate, Sketchbook)
  • Creating simple sketches and illustrations
  • Color theory and digital painting basics
  • Tips for improving your digital artwork
