Book Club Discussion: Sci-Fi Classic

Dive deep into a thought-provoking science fiction classic with fellow book enthusiasts. Share your interpretations, theories, and favorite moments.

Date: 25 July 2025

Time: 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM

Location: Main Reading Room (Level 1)

Book of the Month: "Dune" by Frank Herbert

Host: Ms. Lena Tan

Refreshments: Light refreshments will be provided.

Our discussion will cover themes, character development, world-building, and the lasting impact of this iconic novel. Whether you're a long-time fan or reading it for the first time, all perspectives are welcome!

  • In-depth analysis of the book's themes
  • Character discussions
  • Exploration of the world and lore
  • Open Q&A and sharing of insights
